<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Resources and Tames That Matter Early">

Early efficiency comes from choosing what to ignore. You do not need every shiny option in the first few minutes. You need the smallest set of tools that improves movement, gathering, and survival.

Focus on utility first. If a resource, tame, or tool does not help you move, survive, or finish a mission faster, it can usually wait. That mindset keeps your inventory lean and your decisions sharp.

| Priority | What to Focus On | Why It Wins Early |
|---|---|---|
| High | Basic survival resources | Helps you stay alive and keep moving |
| High | Mission rewards | Turns progress into visible gains |
| Medium | Mobility tools | Makes scouting safer and faster |
| Medium | Reliable transport | Cuts down wasted travel time |
| Low | Specialty combat gear | Better after you have a stable base |
| Low | Endgame tames | Strong later, but not urgent now |
