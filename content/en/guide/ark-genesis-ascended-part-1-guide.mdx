| Biome | Main Threat | Best Use | Notable Content |
|---|---|---|---|

| Ocean | Deep water, whirlpools, waves, buoyancy | Sea travel and bases | Palaeoctopus, Megachelon |
| Arctic | Cold survival, mountain danger | Controlled exploration | Ferox routes |
| Volcanic | Heat and eruption hazards | Risk-heavy progression | Magmasaur utility |

| Route Priority | Recommendation | Reason |
|---|---|---|

| Second | Ocean shoreline routes | Good for travel planning and base ideas |

The safest way to play this DLC is to match your loadout to the route you plan to run. That means fewer wasted trips, fewer dead tames, and better reward flow.

1. Run a mission.
2. Convert the reward into useful supplies.
3. Upgrade your tame or gear.
4. Enter a harder biome only when the route is clear.

That cycle keeps the DLC readable and avoids the common trap of farming in the wrong place for the wrong reward.
