<Section icon={Shield} title="Biomes and Ocean Overhaul">

The map is built around pressure. Each biome pushes a different survival problem, and the ocean overhaul makes water travel feel like a major system rather than a side zone. <Callout type="warning" title="Biome Risk Order">
Do not treat every biome as equal. **Bog and Ocean** are early knowledge checks, while **Volcanic and Lunar** are much harsher when you are undergeared.

<Callout type="success" title="Progression Rule">
Run missions for **Hexagons**, use Hexagons for survival value, then return to harder missions with better tools. That cycle keeps momentum steady.
